Welcome to the Fynd Movie Database. We are still under development, but you can access our APIs at the following URLs:

Movies List, Create: /api/v1/movies/
Movie Retrieve, Edit, Delete: /api/v1/movies/:uuid/

Directors List, Create: /api/v1/directors/
Directors Retrieve, Edit, Delete: /api/v1/directors/:uuid/

Genre List, Create: /api/v1/genres/
Genre Retrieve, Edit, Delete: /api/v1/genres/:uuid/
